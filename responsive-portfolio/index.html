About Me

I am Jasmine Coronel Cacerez, a third-year Computer Science undergraduate at the University of the Philippines Cebu. I’m a curious learner with a growing interest in game development, UI/UX design, and quality assurance. I primarily focus on front-end development and I strive to create intuitive and user-friendly interfaces that avoid redundancy and enhance user experience.

My interest in Computer Science began in high school, where I was introduced to Visual Basic Studio and was inspired to create a mock game as my project. My inclination toward front-end development and visual design also stems from my background as a self-taught artist. When I first entered college, I had no prior experience with programming. However, through the guidance of dedicated professors and a wide range of courses, I’ve since gained hands-on experience with several programming languages. As I continue to grow as a programmer, I’m eager to expand my skills across the development stack while contributing to meaningful, well-designed projects.
